The Luminaries Fan Site

Astral twins Emery Staines and Anna Wetherell are the star-crossed lovers who are cruelly separated after a fleeting meet by chance. They end up fighting every inch of the way of their destiny in rough-and-tough but wildly beautiful New Zealand. Even the land is an important character. Author Eleanor Catton specifically made sure that the flora and fauna of the West Coast of New Zealand was featured in the television series.
